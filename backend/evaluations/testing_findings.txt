
> Follow up questions
-- if multiple UNRELATED questions were asked in the same session, which implied different filters,
the LLM is not sure how to handle the follow up question
---- example: 
---- Q1: "What are the top 5 Services by CSAT driven by AI?" -- includes filter: poweredbyAI = "yes"
---- Q2: "Give me the top 5 services by SLA in 25"
---- Q3: "now give me the CSAT for each of these services for the same period" , answer: considers this as a follow up to Q1, not Q2



> Question ambiguity
-- Some scenarios need query rewriting.
--- example: 
--- "I driven Services by CSAT" - answer: dax is generated without filter poweredbyAI = "yes"
--- “Services by CSAT driven by AI” - answer: dax is generated correctly
